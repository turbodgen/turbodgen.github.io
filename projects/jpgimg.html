Image JPG - 4/13/22 to 4/15/22

I started on April 13, 2022 with a basic idea of converting an image over and over again using Python PIL and using MoviePy to add all of the frames together. I made it so it would turn a PNG into a JPG, to a PNG, to a JPG, over and over again 100 times. It was a lot easier than I thought it was going to be.

All I had to do then was use MoviePy to add all of the images. I decided to use 24 fps. I was basically finished after that! All I did then was make it lower the quality of the image so it takes less time, and added a random music clip over it. That was all!
